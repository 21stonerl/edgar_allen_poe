Figures of Speech List

Figure of Speech Definition Example
metaphor A direct comparison between two things without using 'like' or 'as'. In short, it has been shown that no man can sit down to write without a very profound design
personification Giving human qualities to non-human things or abstract concepts. The world revolves from right to left
imagery Descriptive language that appeals to the senses, often used to create vivid mental images.
simile A comparison between two different things using the word 'like' or 'as'. The fashion had "grown with his growth and strengthened with his strength," so that, when he came to be a man, he could scarcely utter a sentence without interlarding it with a proposition to gamble
hyperbole An exaggerated statement not meant to be taken literally, often for emphasis or effect.
